# PHYS 130B — Research Report Assignment

## Announcement

Each section of the course (§1.1 through §6.4) includes a **research-level project** described on the section parent page of the lecture notes. You are required to complete **one research report** during the quarter.

## Requirements

- **Choose one topic** for your research report. You may either:
  - Pick from the 20 projects listed on the lecture notes (see table below), or
  - Propose your own research problem of **similar style and level** — a question at the frontier of physics that requires literature survey, computation, and scientific writing.
- The report should reflect approximately **two weeks of work** with AI assistance.
- This is **genuine scientific inquiry**, not a large homework problem. Your report should include:
  - A **literature survey** of the relevant research area.
  - **Computation or analytical work** that goes beyond the lecture material.
  - **Scientific writing** that presents your findings clearly, with proper citations.
- You are encouraged to use AI tools throughout the process — for literature search, coding, drafting, and revision. The report should demonstrate your **understanding and judgment**, not just AI output.
- **Grading criteria**: correctness, novelty, clarity of presentation.
